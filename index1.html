    Unemployment Rate

    The Labour Force Survey provides estimates of employment and unemployment. LFS data are used to produce the well-known unemployment rate as well as other standard labour market indicators such as the employment rate and the participation rate. June LFS data reflect labour market conditions during the week of June 13 to 19. Released: 2021-07-09

    Immigrants Rate

    Canada has been a land of immigrants since the first European colonizers of the 16th century, a trend that continues today. The 2016 Census provides a statistical portrait of immigration and ethnocultural diversity in Canada. Immigrants (%) are people who reported they were or had ever been a landed immigrant or permanent resident in Canada.

    Crime Severity Indexes

    Crime Severity Indexes are based on Criminal Code incidents, including traffic offences, as well as other federal statute violations. The base index was set at 100 for 2006 for Canada. Populations are based upon July 1st estimates from Statistics Canada, Centre for Demography. The reference date is January 1, 2011.
